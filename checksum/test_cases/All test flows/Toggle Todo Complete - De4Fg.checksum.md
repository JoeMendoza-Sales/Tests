## Test Steps

1. Clear localStorage and navigate to https://joetodoapp-a03b4.web.app/
2. Type "Toggle this task" in #todoInput and click the "Add" button
3. Verify the todo item is visible with unchecked checkbox and no strikethrough styling
4. Click the checkbox (.todo-checkbox) on the todo item
5. Verify the checkbox is now checked and the todo text has strikethrough styling applied
6. Click the checkbox again to uncheck it
7. Verify the checkbox is unchecked and the strikethrough styling is removed
